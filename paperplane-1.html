Let's make a Paper Aeroplane

A fun paper plane making activity:

If you're looking for printable instructions to make a paper plane, this resource is just what you need.


It includes step by step instructions with pictures to help your children make their own paper plane. You could use this as a fun way to teach Maths or Physics and have your kids consider measurements and flight-path to see how far they can make their paper plane fly.

You will need:

  • a piece of A4-sized paper
  • Opposable thumbs



Let's Begin

  1. Make a line in the centre by folding the paper in half longways then opening it out flat.

  2. Fold in two of the corners so they meet at the centre fold.

  3. Fold the outer edges in again to meet at the centre fold.

  4. Turn the paper over and then fold it in half in the middle.

  5. Fold down the wings on each side so that they meet with the bottom of the plane.

  6. Your plane is ready to fly! How far can you make it go?
